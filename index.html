ABOUT ME

I have always been fascinated with people that push themselves, whether it be climbing the highest mountains or running their first ever 10k.

I grew up in the countryside of Northumberland, the outdoors is my natrual home. It's where I feel alive and comfortable.

Photography and video are the most powerful way of communicating your message. Visuals can send shivers down your spine and engage individuals in ways other mediums can only dream of.

I believe there are so many things in this world that are yet to be done and so many new stories to be told. My goal in life is to capture those incredible moments and ensure they live on forever.
